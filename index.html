My name is Benjamin Fong. I am a first-year student at the University of California Riverside. I am currently a pre-business major, but will be pursuing a bachelor's degree in Business Marketing. I chose to pursue marketing as a concentration because I believe that it is the most interesting. Marketing involves studying how people behave and think and that to me is very intriguing. I hope to learn many things while attending school at UCR and look forward to growing as a student as well as a businessman. While at UCR, I plan to take classes that will help me to succeed in Marketing. Classes like psychology, communications, foreign language, marketing classes, etc. I also want to study abroad so that I can understand how foreign markets work as well.

I have experience working at Boba Guys while living in San Francisco. At Boba Guys, I learned a lot through the founders about consumer behavior and how to influence consumers to buy our product. Boba Guys are known for their instagram presence. By reposting consumers' posts, it often influences consumers to buy the product just to be able to post a picture of their drink. This marketing strategy was very interesting and unique. More and more companies are starting to revolve their marketing around social media and the internet now. It looks like my previous company had an early start.

Personally, I like the presence of others and their thoughts. Figuring out how people think is something that I tend to do everyday and why Marketing is such a great fit for me. I also watch a lot of videos on the internet and play lots of video games, so the creative side of Marketing is also very intriguing to me.
